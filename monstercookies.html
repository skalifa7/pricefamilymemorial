Monster Cookies

This makes a lot of cookies...you'll need a really large bowl.


  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 lb Brown Sugar
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Vanilla
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Corn Syrup
  • 4 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 lb Butter
  • 1 1/2 lb Peanut Butter
  • 8 oz Chocolate Chips
  • 8 oz Plain M&M's
  • 2 Cups White Sugar
  • 9 Cups Uncooked Oatmeal

    • Mix the best way you can with large spoon or spatula.
    Drop by tablespoon or ice cream scoop (6 or 8 per sheet)
    onto cookie sheets. Bake at 350 degrees for 12-14 minutes.
